1. ISO

    ISO is the amount of sensitivity your camera has to light. It is essential to know if you want a good quality photo.

  2. Shutter Speed

    Shutter speed is the amount of time that your camera's shutter remains open to allow light into the lens. It is essential to know.

  3. Aperture

    Aperture allows in more or less light for exposure and controls sharpness. Use aperture to take really nice portrait photos.
